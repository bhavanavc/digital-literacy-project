Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links received via email or SMS
- Always verify website URLs before entering personal information
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details with anyone

(UPI/Financial Safety Tips)
- Do not accept unknown UPI collect requests
- Double-check UPI IDs and payment details before sending money
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions

- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly
- Log out from shared or public devices after use
